About Us

Risu Climbing is a 4,000 square foot gym with over 3,500 square feet of climbing surface with heights up to 21ft. We have terrain for beginners up to advanced climbers, with climbing holds produced by some of the best manufacturers in the country. These include Nicros, eGrips, and Atomik.

There are currently 50+ top rope routes and 20+ bouldering routes. High-intensity training wall & weight training room will be available for those of you training for your next outdoor climbing expeditions. Risu Climbing is the place to begin a new and challenging sport for all ages from toddlers to seniors. Come join us!
